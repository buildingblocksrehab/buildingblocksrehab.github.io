Special Education

Speech Therapy

Speech Therapy include articulation therapy, language intervention activities, and others depending on the type of speech or language disorder.

Physical Therapy

We treat pain arising from musculoskeletal conditions, injury and post surgical cases. We manage posture, muscle imbalance cases, neurology, neurosurgical conditions, gait and imbalance conditions. We treat movement disorders and specialise in cardiopulmonary rehabilitation.

We also provide pre and post natal exercise, pelvic floor strengthening for pregnancy care and other gynaecology conditions and specialise in oncology care and lymphaedema management

Our Treatment Includes :

Pain management, Dry needling, Cupping, Sports taping, Manual therapy, Deep heating, Pneumatic Compression therapy, Paraffin wax therapy, IFT, tens, Muscle stimulation, Ultrasound, Cervical and lumbar traction, Hand strength training and rehabilitation, Balance training, General fitness and exercises.

Occupation Therapy

Occupational therapy can benefit children with learning and behavioral disorders, developmental delays, visual and hearing impairments, emotional disturbances or physical disabilities. They may learn to function more freely in a developmental or sensory integration program.

The purpose of therapy is to help strengthen the coordination between brain and body to gain and/or enhance function in daily living.

  • Areas we deal in

  • Sensory processing skills

  • Fine & Gross motor skills

  • Visual Perception

  • Memory & Cognition

  • Handwriting Difficulties

  • School Readiness

  • Play & Group therapy
